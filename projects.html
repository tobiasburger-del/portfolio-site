CanSat Competition - SpaceZ

My team, SpaceZ, engineered a satellite the size of a soda can. We reached the finals and witnessed our satellite's successful return after launching in a real rocket. ESA and NSO founded this competition in collaboration with TU Delft and NEMO. We secured sponsorship from NLR (Royal Netherlands Aerospace Centre) and even featured in a newscast. In this project, I was the team leader.
